About River Valley Roleplay

River Valley Roleplay is a whitelisted ERLC / FiveM Roleplay community dedicated to providing a professional, serious, and immersive roleplaying experience. We prioritize realistic roleplay, consistency, and staff-led training.

Our Mission

To create a respectful and realistic ERLC environment where players can develop character, improve roleplay skills, and participate in well-structured operations.

How we operate

  • Strict whitelisting — applications reviewed by staff
  • FTO & training nights for all emergency services
  • Clear chain of command and promotion system

Join Us

We run regular training, community events, and recruitment drives. If you want serious ERLC RP, start by applying or joining our Discord.
